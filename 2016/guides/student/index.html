Transportation

If you are flying into Sea-Tac Airport…

  1. The most cost efficient option is to use the Link Light Rail Public Transit System. This train will bring you to campus for less than five dollars. The SeaTac Airport Station is connected to the fourth floor of the airport parking garage. The Link Light Rail service runs from 5:00 a.m. to 1:00 a.m. Monday through Saturday and 6:00 a.m. to midnight on Sundays.  

  2. Lyft and Uber also provide services at Sea-Tac Airport. Once you have arranged for your ride, proceed to the Parking Garage Ground Transportation Plaza on the 3rd floor. Then, follow the signs to the TNC/Rideshare (Lyft, Uber) pickup area in Sections I & J.


If you were notified about receiving travel reimbursement...

Please remember that all receipts for airfare and ground transportation must be emailed to finance@dubhacks.co no later than October 21, 2016. Please allow several weeks for the reimbursements to be processed.


If you are driving...

Parking on campus is complimentary from noon on Saturday to 6:00 am on Monday. The Central Plaza Garage is nearest to the DubHacks venue halls.
 

For the Weekend

What should I bring?

The overnight essentials such as a laptop, chargers, a valid ID, sleeping bag, etc.

 

What resources will I have access to?

Tech mentors will be available throughout the event to assist you with both the brainstorming and development of your projects. They will be there to answer any questions you may have regarding specific technologies or to get feedback on your projects. A hardware lab will also be available for use.
