Transportation

From Sea-Tac Airport

The most cost efficient option is to use the Link Light Rail Public Transit System. This train will bring you to campus for less than five dollars. The SeaTac Airport Station is connected to the fourth floor of the airport parking garage. The Link Light Rail service runs from 5:00 a.m. to 1:00 a.m. Monday through Saturday and 6:00 a.m. to midnight on Sundays.

Lyft and Uber also provide services at Sea-Tac Airport. Once you have arranged for your ride, proceed to the Parking Garage Ground Transportation Plaza on the 3rd floor. Then, follow the signs to the TNC/Rideshare (Lyft, Uber) pickup area in Sections I & J.

Parking

Parking on campus is complimentary from 12:00pm on Saturday until 6:00am on Monday. There is a parking garage (Central Plaza Garage) under Kane Hall.

Judging Criteria

After all the submissions are in, we will send you a table number and room number to go to for expo judging. Please get there as soon as you can and set up quickly.

These are the following criteria you will be judged on:

  • Technical Complexity
  • Execution and Usability
  • How well you fulfill your challenge

Presentations will be around 2-3 minutes, and you should aim to have room for your judge to ask questions.
